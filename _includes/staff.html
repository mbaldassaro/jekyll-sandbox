Eric Bjornlund

Eric Bjornlund is co-founder and principal of Democracy International. A lawyer and development professional with two decades of international experience, Mr. Bjornlund has designed, managed, and evaluated democratic development programs in 30 countries in Africa, Asia, Europe, Eurasia, and the Middle East.




Glenn Cowan




Glenn Cowan is a co-founder and principal of Democracy International. He is an expert on public opinion research, political organizing, advocacy, legislative processes, local government, election administration and monitoring, and vote count verification. Over the past three decades, he has advised government agencies, legislatures, political parties, candidates, trade associations, and corporate and nonprofit clients in the U.S. and around the world.
